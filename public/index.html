About Us

For more than 30 years in North Africa and the Middle East, we took the decision to start a new mission by opening a new contracting company in Dubai; Awlad Salem Co.L.L.C. Eng. Mohamed Abdel Fattah Salem has created his first private company in Egypt under the name of MHDA Co.L.L.C. since 1994 to work in several sectors in construction.

But Now

Now in 2015, the world has recognised the big rise in construction in UAE and specifically Dubai, due to the EXPO 2020. Hoping, to use our past experience in UAE ́s market, wishing to have our part in contracting residential, steel structures, warehouses and factories to perform as we always did, professionally and sticking to our plans and time schedules. So, Awlad Salem Co. L.L.C. is at your disposal to participate in the rise.

MHDA Co. L.L.C.

With the will of Eng. Mohamed Abdel Fattah Salem, our main branch was established in 1994, as a company taking part of the Construction, Industrial and Electromechanical, market, which was registered in Egypt under its laws. Soon after, in 2001 MHDA Co. L.L.C. opened it's branch in Algeria and was registered with the Algerian authorities under the name of SAR.
Furthermore, in 2002 MHDA Co. L.L.C. once again was able to expand into Libya under the Libyan laws with the name of MHDA Libya.
